About Boss Plumbing

Founded in 2024 by Danny Brennen, Boss Plumbing was born out of a passion for providing reliable, affordable plumbing services to the Bakersfield community. With over 10 years of hands-on experience, Danny has built his expertise by working in every corner of the plumbing industry, from residential repairs to complex commercial projects.

What started as a small dream has grown into a trusted local business, rooted in the values of honesty, integrity, and hard work. Danny’s journey is one of determination — a self-made individual who, coming from humble beginnings, took the leap to start his own company. His mission is simple: to offer top-notch plumbing services while maintaining a personal touch with every client.

At Boss Plumbing, we’re not just about fixing pipes. We’re about building lasting relationships with our customers and ensuring that every job, no matter how big or small, is done right the first time. Danny’s commitment to excellence and his dedication to the community are at the heart of everything we do.

Whether you're dealing with a leaky faucet, a water heater malfunction, or a complete plumbing overhaul, Boss Plumbing is here to provide the service you can count on. We pride ourselves on our reputation as the go-to plumber in Bakersfield — a business built on trust and results.
